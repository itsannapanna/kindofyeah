About the Bootcamp

This Startup Bootcamp facilitates aspiring entrepreneurs in acquiring the skills to assess and select viable startup ideas, navigate the journey from idea to company formation, and enhance the likelihood of establishing a thriving and successful startup.

Bootcamp Goal

Encompassing a wide range of subjects, this comprehensive bootcamp delves into critical areas such as assessing startup ideas, crafting a minimum viable product, implementing effective monetization strategies, understanding the fundamentals of finance and legal aspects for startup founders, and much more.

Course Format

The course is structured into multiple modules, each accompanied by a set of assignments directly aligned with the course content.

Open to all

This course is open to students from NIT Jamshedpur and individuals from other institutions, ensuring accessibility to a broader audience.
